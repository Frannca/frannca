About

In activity since 2015, the company story started to be written a couple years before. When the founder got the first clients. Always seeking to improve your skills, the first projects were simple, but with focus in the clients needs. Before the foundation, Webhost demonstrated interest in forming a partnership for the development of projects. The partnership showed a promising future, and in the same year the Frannca company was founded.

Following the evolution of web development technologies and seeking to offer the best solutions to its customers, in 2017 the company started working with Angular and API. The improvements also extended to usability, using Semantic UI instead of Bootstrap. Always seeking to bring innovative solutions.
